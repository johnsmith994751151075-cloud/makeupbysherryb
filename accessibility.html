Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 1, 2026  ·  MakeupbySherryb LLC

Partially Conformant — WCAG 2.1 AA

Our Commitment

MakeupbySherryb LLC is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and continually improve the experience for everyone.

Conformance Status

This website is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. We are actively working toward full conformance and welcome feedback from our visitors.

Measures Taken

  • Skip navigation link at the top of every page for keyboard users
  • All images include descriptive alternative text
  • All form fields are associated with visible, descriptive labels
  • ARIA landmark roles used (navigation, main, footer) for screen reader navigation
  • Consistent heading structure (H1 → H2 → H3) throughout all pages
  • HTML lang="en" declared on all pages
  • Keyboard-accessible cookie consent banner and navigation
  • Focus indicators visible on all interactive elements
  • Animations and transitions respect the prefers-reduced-motion user preference
  • Color contrast meets or exceeds WCAG 2.1 AA for primary body text
  • No content flashes or strobing effects

Known Limitations

We are aware of the following areas that may not yet fully meet WCAG 2.1 AA:

  • Portfolio gallery lightbox: keyboard navigation within the lightbox may be limited on some browsers
  • Custom cursor: a standard cursor is preserved for users who rely on system cursor settings
  • Some decorative images may have empty alt attributes — these are intentionally left blank as they do not convey meaningful content

We are actively working to address these limitations.

Technical Specifications

This site is built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Accessibility features rely on WAI-ARIA specifications. Tested with keyboard-only navigation, VoiceOver (macOS/iOS), and NVDA (Windows).

Feedback & Contact

Your feedback helps us improve. If you encounter an accessibility barrier on our website, please let us know:

  • Email: hello@makeupbysherryb.com
  • Response time: We will respond within 2 business days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the U.S. Department of Justice ADA Information Line at 1-800-514-0301.

Formal Complaints

MakeupbySherryb LLC is committed to resolving accessibility concerns in good faith. We ask that you contact us directly at hello@makeupbysherryb.com before initiating any formal legal process. We are prepared to work collaboratively toward an accessible experience for all users.

Date of Assessment

This statement was prepared on June 1, 2026 and reflects the current state of the website at that time. We review and update this statement at least annually.
