The Artist

Beauty that starts
with knowing you.

I'm Sherry B — a South Asian bridal makeup artist based in Atlanta, Georgia, with over a decade of experience creating looks that feel as good as they photograph. Whether you're walking down the aisle in a lehenga, a sari, or something entirely your own, I approach every bride as a singular work of art.

My work bridges traditional South Asian techniques and modern luxury artistry — rich, pigmented bridal looks that last 16+ hours and still feel like you. Not a mask. Not a filter. Just the most beautiful version of yourself.
