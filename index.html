I'm Sherry B. I grew up immersed in South Asian celebrations — the weight of a lehenga, the warmth of gold, the way a bride's face should carry every emotion of the day without flinching.

Based in Atlanta, I specialize in bridal artistry for South Asian women who want a look that honors where they come from and reflects exactly who they are. Traditional. Fusion. Always personal.
