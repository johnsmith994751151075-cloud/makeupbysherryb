The Artist

Beauty rooted in
culture. Elevated
by craft.

I'm Sherry B. I grew up surrounded by South Asian celebrations — the weight of a lehenga, the scent of rose water, the way a bride's eyes should look when she sees herself for the first time. That's not something you learn. That's something you carry.

Based in Atlanta, I specialize in bridal artistry for South Asian women who want a look that honors their heritage and reflects who they actually are. Not a formula. Not a template. A face that belongs to the person wearing it — just elevated.
