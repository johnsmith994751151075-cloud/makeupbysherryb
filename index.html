The Artist

Beauty rooted in culture,
elevated by craft.

I'm Sherry B — a makeup artist based in Atlanta with a deep passion for South Asian bridal beauty. I grew up immersed in the richness of South Asian celebrations: the colors, the traditions, the emotion. That understanding lives in my work.

My clients are brides who want a look that honors their heritage while reflecting who they are today — whether that's a full traditional Dulhan, a contemporary fusion, or something entirely their own. I listen first. Then I create.

Every face is different. Every wedding is different. I don't apply a formula — I build a look that belongs to you.

Questions

Things brides ask me

How far in advance should I book? +
South Asian wedding season fills up quickly. I recommend booking 6–12 months in advance for peak dates (April–June and October–December). Reach out as soon as you have a date — I'll let you know availability honestly.
Do you offer airbrush makeup? +
Yes. I offer both traditional and airbrush application. We'll discuss which is best for your skin type, the look you want, and your venue's lighting during your consultation.
Do you travel outside Atlanta? +
Absolutely. I'm based in Atlanta but available for destination weddings domestically and internationally. A travel fee applies — reach out with your location and I'll put together a quote.
What should I expect at the trial? +
The trial is where we build your look together. Bring inspiration photos, any dupatta or jewelry you're considering, and your honest feedback. We refine until it's exactly right. Trials are typically done 1–3 months before the wedding.
Do you work with all South Asian skin tones? +
Always. I work across the full spectrum of South Asian skin tones and undertones and carry a curated kit built specifically for deeper complexions. Every bride deserves a look that photographs true to her skin.
