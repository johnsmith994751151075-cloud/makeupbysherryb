This Privacy Policy describes how MakeupbySherryb LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information submitted through this website (makeupbysherryb.com). We respect your privacy and are committed to compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and applicable global privacy laws.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

The data controller for personal information collected through this website is:

  • Business: MakeupbySherryb LLC
  • Location: Atlanta, Georgia, United States
  • Website: makeupbysherryb.com
  • Privacy contact: hello@makeupbysherryb.com

For any privacy-related questions or rights requests, please email hello@makeupbysherryb.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily submit it through our booking inquiry form. We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data.

Booking Inquiry Form — we may collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Wedding or event date
  • Requested ready-by time
  • Venue or location name
  • Service type requested
  • Vision or message you provide (optional)

Automatically Collected Technical Data:

When you visit this website, third-party services (described in Section 6) may automatically collect limited technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and referring URL as part of their standard operation. We do not independently collect or store server access logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively for:

  • Responding to booking inquiries: To contact you about your submission, typically within 48 hours.
  • Scheduling and coordination: To coordinate availability, date holds, and service details for your event.
  • Service delivery: To provide the makeup artistry services you have requested or contracted.
  • Follow-up communications: To send information relevant to your booked or inquired service (e.g., trial reminders, preparation tips).

We will not add you to any mailing list or send you promotional communications without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For individuals in the EEA or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Pre-contractual steps (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to respond to your inquiry and take steps toward a service agreement at your request.
  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to perform a contract for services you have engaged us to provide.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): Responding to direct inquiries submitted by you. We have assessed that our legitimate interest does not override your rights.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): For any optional communications (e.g., promotional emails), we will obtain your separate, explicit consent first.

5. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and client records for up to 3 years following your inquiry or the date of services rendered (whichever is later). This period is necessary for business record-keeping, dispute resolution, and any applicable tax or legal requirements.

If you request deletion of your data before this period expires, we will comply within 30 days except where retention is required by applicable law.

6. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We share your data only with the minimal set of service providers necessary to operate this website and deliver services. We do not sell or rent your data to any third party.

  • Google Fonts: This website loads typefaces from Google Fonts, which causes your browser to make requests to Google servers. Google may log your IP address as part of this process. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (venue autocomplete): When you type a venue or location into the booking form, the website makes requests to the OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoding service. This involves your typed search query and your IP address being sent to OpenStreetMap servers. See the OSM Foundation Privacy Policy.
  • Instagram (portfolio link): Our website links to our Instagram profile (@makeupbysherryb). Clicking that link takes you to Instagram's platform, subject to Instagram's Privacy Policy. We do not embed Instagram feeds that load content from Instagram servers automatically.

7. International Data Transfers

We are based in the United States. If you are located in the EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction with data transfer restrictions, your data may be processed in the US, which may not offer equivalent data protection to your home jurisdiction.

For EEA/UK residents, where transfers occur we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). Our service providers (Google, OpenStreetMap) each maintain their own transfer compliance mechanisms. You may contact us for details.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We do not set any first-party cookies on this website, and we do not use cookies for analytics or advertising tracking.

Third-party services (such as Google Fonts and OpenStreetMap) may set cookies or use browser fingerprinting as part of their own operations. You can manage third-party cookies in your browser settings. Since we do not use non-essential cookies ourselves, we have not implemented a cookie consent banner — but we will do so if our use of third-party services changes.

9. Your Rights

Right of Access (GDPR / CCPA) Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
Right to Rectification (GDPR) Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Right to Erasure (GDPR / CCPA) Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions.
Right to Restriction (GDPR) Request restricted processing in certain circumstances.
Right to Portability (GDPR) Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Right to Object (GDPR) Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
No Sale of Data (CCPA) We do not sell your data. No opt-out mechanism is required.
Non-Discrimination (CCPA) Exercising your rights will not affect the quality of our services.

To exercise any right, email hello@makeupbysherryb.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA). We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
