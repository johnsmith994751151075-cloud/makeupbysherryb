MakeupbySherryb LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through makeupbysherryb.com. We are committed to compliance with applicable U.S. privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) and the CAN-SPAM Act.

1. Who We Are

The data controller for information collected through this website is:

  • Business: MakeupbySherryb LLC
  • Location: Atlanta, Georgia, United States
  • Website: makeupbysherryb.com
  • Contact: info@makeupbysherryb.com

2. Information We Collect via Contact & Booking Form

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily submit it through our inquiry or booking form. We may collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Wedding or event date
  • Venue / location
  • Vision / style notes you choose to share
  • Service type requested

Purpose: To respond to your inquiry, assess availability, and provide the makeup artistry services you've requested.
Legal basis: Your consent (you submit the form voluntarily) and the steps necessary to enter a service agreement.

3. Third-Party Tools

Your form data may be processed by the following third-party tools we use to operate our business:

  • Email client (Gmail): Inquiry responses are sent and managed through Gmail. Your submitted data may pass through Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Booking tools (HoneyBook, Calendly, or equivalent): If applicable, booking confirmations and contract management may be handled via a third-party booking platform. These processors are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Google Fonts & OpenStreetMap: These services load on our website and may receive your IP address as part of standard operation.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Ever.

4. Marketing Communications & CAN-SPAM

If you provide your email address, we may send follow-up information about our services relevant to your inquiry. We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act:

  • Every marketing email includes our business name and contact information.
  • Every marketing email includes a clear and easy way to opt out.
  • You may opt out at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email or contacting us at info@makeupbysherryb.com.
  • We will honor opt-out requests within 10 business days.

We will not add you to any mailing list or send promotional communications without your consent.

5. Data Retention

  • Inquiry data: Retained for up to 2 years for business purposes, or until you request deletion.
  • Booking and contract records: May be retained longer as required by Georgia law for tax and accounting purposes.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (subject to legal retention requirements).

6. Your Rights — Georgia & CCPA

Right to Know Request disclosure of what personal information we hold about you and how it is used.
Right to Delete Request deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal retention requirements.
Right to Correct Request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold.
Opt Out of Sale We do not sell your data. No opt-out action is required.
Non-Discrimination Exercising your rights will not affect the quality of our services.
Opt Out of Marketing Reply "unsubscribe" to any email or email us directly at any time.

To exercise any right, contact us at info@makeupbysherryb.com. We will respond within 30 days (or 45 days for CCPA requests). We may verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): Categories of personal information we collect — Identifiers (name, email, phone), commercial information (services requested, event date), and internet activity (IP address via third-party services). Purpose: booking inquiry response and service delivery. We do not sell personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults (18+) or minors booking with parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 without consent from a parent or legal guardian. If you believe we have inadvertently received data from a minor, please contact us at info@makeupbysherryb.com and we will delete it promptly.

All data submitted through our website is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Access to client data is limited strictly to the business owner.
